How to Submit an Icon

First, please tell us what kind of icon you are uploading. Do any of the following groups (under Library) match your icon? Some have more specific groups inside once you select them.

If you don't see a matching group, you may create one by choosing "Add Group Here" once you find a good place for the group to hold your icon.

If you do see a matching group, click it and choose "Add Icon Here."

Thank you for contributing to iconSyntax!
